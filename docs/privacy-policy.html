Apart from this, information that your device browser sends automatically may be collected whenever you visit this service (when this application checks for updates for example) which contains information, including, but not limited to, the type of mobile device You use, Your mobile device unique ID, the IP address of Your mobile device, Your mobile operating system, the type of mobile Internet browser You use, unique device identifiers and other diagnostic data.
